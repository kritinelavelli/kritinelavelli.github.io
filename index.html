I lead design and prototyping on projects for user studies and design explorations. Check back soon to see some of my work.

I build prototypes for A/B testing, interviews, cognitive walkthroughs and maintain a framework of utils, tools and processes for fast prototyping. I build and maintain a prototype component library that can be used for mixed-fidelity prototyping and prototyping during design sprints. As a UXE I also work with a cross-functional and cross-org team of UXers to build tools to support design systems across Google.

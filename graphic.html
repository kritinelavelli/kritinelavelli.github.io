Flat Design Icon

This flat icon is for a phone app specifically for the android platform. The style is inspired by design principles that Google currently follows. It is in keeping with the Material design initiative which includes minimalistic design, a flat color palette and the use of shadow.

Website Theme

I designed this website for a dancer friend who was about to start his own calisthenics and fitness program. I designed the theme to have a grungy dark look to represent the underground background that he had being a street dancer.


Wedding Motif

My very first client was my sister when she asked me to help organize her wedding. I made this motif to go on the wedding stationary and decoration. This is a motif drawn in the shape of Ganesh the Indian God of prosperity. His picture is printed on wedding invitations and hung in wedding halls for good luck. This motif is unique as it also contains the names of the bride and groom 'Deepti' and 'Indranil'.

Geometric Portrait

This was the result of a 16 hour session with photoshop. It is a vector illustration drawn with geometric shapes using the pen tool. A photograph is used as a guide and shapes are drawn and filled with different colors. Shadows are created using different hues. The light effects on the side of the face is achieved by spliting all shapes using a line through the middle. Further different colors are filled in to achieve this effect.
